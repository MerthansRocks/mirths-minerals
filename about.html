About Us

Hello, my name is Merthan, and I am the owner of Mirth's Minerals. As an army veteran, I honed skills such as attention to detail, discipline, and patience, which have proved invaluable in my passion for creating jewelry pieces out of precious gemstones. Through self-study and hands-on experience, I have gained extensive knowledge about gemstones and their properties, allowing me to create unique designs that set my jewelry apart from others in the industry. I am proud to offer my customers personalized and one-of-a-kind pieces that showcase the beauty and uniqueness of each gemstone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality jewelry pieces made from precious gemstones. We strive to create unique designs that showcase the beauty and uniqueness of each gemstone.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading provider of handcrafted jewelry pieces made from precious gemstones. We strive to create unique designs that showcase the beauty and uniqueness of each gemstone.

Our Values

We value our customers and strive to provide them with the highest quality jewelry pieces. We also value our employees and strive to provide them with a safe and healthy work environment.

Our History

Mirth's Minerals was founded in 2019 by Merthan Mirth. Merthan started the business as a hobby, but it quickly grew into a passion and a full-time job. Merthan has been creating jewelry pieces out of precious gemstones for over 10 years.
